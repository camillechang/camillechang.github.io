First SAP-C02 Experience

  • Scored 728，just need one more question right then I can pass. The main reason is that after quickly finished, I did not check all the question again in case I miss something.
  • 再做对一道题就能过了。主要是做了一遍之后，没有检查，以为下次免费的时候再考。
  • 考试使用中文版，感觉更容易阅读，同时可以切换英文，很方便。
  • 实际真题中会包括一些平时的练习题
  • Exam topics:
    • route53 inbound/outbound
    • Not much new services
    • RTO,RPO, backup and cost related
  • 还是要多练，熟悉并理解自己不懂的知识点
  • You still need to practice more, familiarize yourself knowledge blind spot。
  • After finished exam, I realized that I didnot add voucher in, I cannot have retake next time. Sooooo Sad….

Second SAP-C02 Experience

2/1/2023, had my second exam and passed. After did some hundreds of practice question, I found that there are a few knowledge points that I can’t understand at all, so I just gave up that part.

  • Still confused about aws network advance.

  • 做了几遍之后，发现主要都是 on-prem 到 aws,或者对当前的系统就行优化的，同时也要考虑 cost.

  • 第二次比第一次难得，但是这次真的 2 个小时做完了，然后又一道道检查了一遍。

  • The second time is harder than the first time, but this time after I finished all the questions, and I checked it again and make sure I tried my best. Because after this exam, I had enough aws exams this year.

  • 考完之后，觉得轻松了，不管过不过，我尽力了，得把时间分到其他的上面了。

  • After the exam, I felt relaxed. I didn’t care about passing it, but I tried my best and had to focus on other things now.

  • 但是感觉很多题都只是知识点，除非真的用到了，否则感觉很没用。这个证书听着挺唬人的，其实一堆的服务，有可能一辈子都用不到。

  • To sum up, I feel that many questions are just knowledge points. Unless they are really used, they feel very useless. This certificate sounds bluffing, but in fact, in my whole life I think max 50% of the services will be used.

    1 Udemy practice exams from Neal Davis
    2 Udemy course from Stephane Maarek
    3 Turorials DOJO: AWS Certified Solutions Architect Professional Practice Exams 2022
