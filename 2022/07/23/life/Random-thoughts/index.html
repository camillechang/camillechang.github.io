Reflections on Life 生活随想
Camille Chang
  2022-07-23 19:29:41 2022-07-23 19:29  
  • Life 
 
  • Life 
 337 Words  1 Mins

One

开始清理以前的东西，翻到自己曾经的日记，2012.10.9这天，我写下了想去国外读书，但是没钱没有好的成绩的顾虑。

Starting to clean up old things, I came across my old diary. On October 9th, 2012, I wrote about wanting to study abroad, but I had concerns about not having enough money and not having good grades.

现在回头来看，靠着工作了几年的积蓄和在国外兼职，我已经读完研究生，并且尝试了三年自己喜欢的工作，最后因为薪水太低不得不放弃，而选择了专业对口的工作。真想对当时的自己说，过好当下，脚踏实地，你想要的都会慢慢实现的。

Now looking back, relying on several years of savings from work and part-time jobs abroad, I have completed my graduate studies and pursued the job I was passionate about for three years. In the end, due to the low salary, I had to give it up and chose a job that aligned with my major. I really wish I could tell my past self to live in the moment, stay grounded, and that everything you want will gradually come to fruition.

Two

学会把握每个当下，到最后我们往往会发现，正是那一个又一个当下的累积，圆满了我们的过去，成就了我们的未来。

Learning to seize each moment, in the end, we often realize that it’s the accumulation of these moments that completes our past and shapes our future.
