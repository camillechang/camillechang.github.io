Random thoughts 生活随想
Camille Chang
  2022-07-23 19:29:41 2022-07-23 19:29  330 Words  1 Mins

One

开始清理以前的东西，翻到自己曾经的日记，2012.10.9这天，我写下了想去国外读书，但是没钱没有好的成绩的顾虑。

I started to pack up my old things and opened my old diary. On October 9, 2012, I wrote down that I want to go to other coutry to study, to master an different language and experience lift there, but I had no money and did not graduate from a good school.

现在回头来看，靠着在国内的积蓄和这边打工，已经读完研究生，尝试了三年自己喜欢的工作，最后因为薪水太低，选择了专业对口的工作。真想对当时的自己说，过好当下，脚踏实地，你想要的都会慢慢实现的。

Looking back now, relying on my savings in my own Country by working hard and work part time as childcare educator in Australia, I earned my degree. At the same time, I have found a corresponding job through my own efforts. I really want to say to myself at that time, live the moment well, keep your feet on the ground, and what you want will be realized slowly.

Two

学会把握每个当下，到最后我们往往会发现，正是那一个又一个当下的累积，圆满了我们的过去，成就了我们的未来。

Learn to grasp each moment, and in the end we often find that it is the accumulation of one moment after another that completes our past and fulfills our future.
