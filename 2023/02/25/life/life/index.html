Life - in memory of my beloved uncle 25/2/2023

  • 在人生的前半场，从没想过人生的意义，只是按部就班的生活着，等待着长大，上学工作结婚生子，仿佛人生本就该如此。
  • In the first half of life, I never thought about the meaning of life. I just lived according to the routine, waiting to grow up, go to school, work, get married, and have children, as if this is how life is supposed to be.
  • 很小的时候，见过很多人的生死，所以特别害怕自己死了。但凡看了一点恐怖的电视剧，就晚上吓得不敢出门，不敢睡觉。
  • When I was very young, I witnessed the death of many people, which made me particularly afraid of my own death. After watching even a slightly scary TV show, I would be too scared to go out or sleep at night.
  • 不是学霸，也不是名校毕业，工作也一般般，整个人就特别自卑，特别想认识很多人，想了解这个世界。世界的模样都是听别人说的。
  • I’m not a top student or a graduate from a prestigious university, and my job is just average. I feel particularly insecure about myself and I really want to meet many people and learn about the world. I’ve only heard about what the world looks like from others.
  • 去了国外，才发现不同人生的精彩，原来上学不是都必须学思想政治课的，原来你的长相普通，很多人也会说你漂亮。无论你从事什么工作，都会受到尊重。
  • After visit to other countries, I realized that there are many different and exciting ways of life. I found out that not all schools require students to take courses on ideology and politics. I also discovered that even if you have an ordinary appearance, many people will think your are pretty. Regardless of the type of work you do, you will receive respect.
  • 开始捡起自己破烂的英语，开始慢慢的融入社会。真的融入，原来想得到什么还是需要靠自己不断努力的。
  • started picking up my broken English and slowly integrating into society. I realized that in order to achieve what I wanted, I needed to continuously work hard on my own, even when I had support. I truly became a part of society, and it was through my own effort.
  • 也开始自己爱自己，开始跟以前从未想过的帅哥们约会，开始发现人生让自己开心才最重要，没有必要活在他人的眼中。也就失去了想去了解他人的欲望。
  • I also started loving myself and dating handsome guys that I never thought I would before. I began to realize that it is most important to live a life that makes myself happy, rather than living up to the expectations of others. As a result, I lost the desire to explore others people’s life.
  • 虽然还是没有钱，但是健康，快乐。
  • Although I still don’t have much money, I am healthy and happy.
  • 越长大，越爱自己，也突然意识到，时间是最残忍的，昨天还鲜活的人，今天就已经去了另一个世界，仿佛从来没有存在过。明星政要，不在了，至少还有很多记录。而普通人，消逝了，就像一滴水汇入了大海，再也不见踪影。
  • As I continue to grow older, I love myself more and more. However, I realized that time is the cruelest thing. People who were alive and well yesterday have now left this world and seem to have never existed. Celebrities and politicians who have passed away still have many records left behind. But for ordinary people, once they’re gone, it’s like a drop of water merging into the sea, never to be seen again.
  • 原来人生的长河中，躲不掉还是死亡。把你爱的人和你隔开，仿佛你们之间发生的一切，只是一场梦。
  • It turns out that in the river of life, we cannot escape death. Being separated from someone you love feels like everything that happened between you two was just a dream.
  • 人生有时真的很累，控制不了生死，控制不了疾病，控制不了财富。活了真么久，还是不太会应对死亡。
  • Life can be really exhausting sometimes. We cannot control life and death, sickness, or wealth. Even after living for a long time, we still struggle to come cope with death.
  • 人生苦短，及时行乐，诚不欺我。
  • Life is short, so we should enjoy it while we can. This is something that we should be honest with ourselves about.
